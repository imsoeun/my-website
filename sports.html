Samsung Lions

Samsung Lions are a professional baseball team based in South Korea and are one of the most successful teams in the KBO League. Founded in 1982, they have won multiple Korean Series championships and are known for their strong fanbase and rich history.

Los Angeles Dodgers

Los Angeles Dodgers are a Major League Baseball (MLB) team based in Los Angeles, California. With a legacy dating back to 1883, they have won multiple World Series titles and are recognized for their iconic blue and white uniforms, as well as their historic rivalry with the San Francisco Giants.

Los Angeles Lakers

Los Angeles Lakers are a professional basketball team in the NBA, known for their storied history and legendary players such as Magic Johnson, Kobe Bryant, and LeBron James. Based in Los Angeles, California, they have won numerous NBA championships and are regarded as one of the most successful franchises in basketball history.

Real Madrid

Real Madrid is one of the most prestigious football clubs in the world, based in Madrid, Spain. With a record number of UEFA Champions League titles, they are known for their world-class players, fierce rivalry with FC Barcelona, and their impact on global football culture.

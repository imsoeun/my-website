  • Daebak is a brown, cute toy poodle.

Daebak became a part of our family in 2014, and ever since then, he has been the beloved youngest member of our home. With his bright, cheerful smile, he has a special way of healing us. Whenever we feel down or overwhelmed, simply petting Daebak instantly lifts our spirits. His warmth and energy bring happiness to everyone around him, making every moment with him truly comforting.

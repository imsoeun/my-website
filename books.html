One of my favorite hobbies is reading. I enjoy books from all genres, but lately, I’ve been drawn to those that provide practical knowledge about life rather than literature. This year, I’ve set a goal to read one book per month, and for March, I’m currently reading The Psychology of Money by Morgan Housel. Among all the books I’ve read, my all-time favorite is The Brave New World by Aldous Huxley, a thought-provoking dystopian novel that left a lasting impression on me. I also enjoy comparing original texts with their translations. For example, I’ve read The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry in French, English, and Korean to explore the subtle nuances across languages.
